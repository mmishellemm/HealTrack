HealTrack

Welcome to HealTrack!

HealTrack is a web application designed to help you track your symptoms, manage your health records, and connect with healthcare professionals as well as insurance companies.

Tracker

This tool lets you log and track your symptoms in real time. You can easily record changes and keep a full history to share with your doctor, ensuring nothing is forgotten during your visits.

Records

This feature enables you to upload and securely store important medical documents, including test results, imaging, and other records. It ensures you have easy access to all your medical information in one place, making it convenient to share with new doctors and maintain a comprehensive health history.

Physician and Coverage

Find doctors that works for YOU! Filter through different languages, specialties, and more to find your best fit and see if they take most insurances.

Helplines

If you or a loved one are struggling, look through these resources to ensure you get the help you need. If you are unsure about what you are experiencing, this page will help you learn more.
